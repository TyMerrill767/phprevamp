The Band

Thomas Bangalter

Thomas Bangalter is a French electronic musician best known for being one half of the French house music duo Daft Punk, alongside Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo. He has also recorded and released music as a member of the trio Stardust, the duo Together, and as a solo artist including compositions for the film Irréversible. Thomas Bangalter owns a music label called Roulé. Outside of music production, his credits include film director and cinematographer.

Thomas was born in Paris, France. He began playing the piano at the age of six. Bangalter stated in a video interview that his parents were strict in keeping up his practice, for which he later thanked them. His father, Daniel Vangarde was a famous songwriter and producer for performers such as the Gibson Brothers, Ottawan, and Sheila B. Devotion.

Guy Manuel de Homem-Christo

Guillaume Emmanuel "Guy-Manuel" de Homem-Christo is the other half of the French house music duo Daft Punk. He has also produced several works from his record label Crydamoure with label co-owner Éric Chedeville. He and Chedeville formed the musical duo Le Knight Club.

De Homem-Christo met Thomas Bangalter when they were attending the Lycée Carnot school together in 1987. It was there that they discovered their mutual fascination for films and music of the 1960s and 70s. A negative review referred to their music as "a daft punky thrash", which inspired the band's new name.
